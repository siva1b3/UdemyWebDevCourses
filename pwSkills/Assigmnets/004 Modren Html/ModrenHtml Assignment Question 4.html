Improving Accessibility of HTML

  • 1. Use Semantic HTML: Utilize appropriate semantic HTML elements to convey the structure and meaning of the content, aiding assistive technologies and improving overall accessibility.
  • 2. Provide Alternative Text (Alt Text): Always include descriptive alt text for images, providing meaningful descriptions to users who rely on screen readers or cannot view the images.
  • 3. Ensure Keyboard Accessibility: Make sure all interactive elements and functionalities are accessible and navigable using a keyboard alone, ensuring usability for individuals with motor impairments or those who rely on keyboard navigation.
